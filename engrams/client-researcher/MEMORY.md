## Sector Notes

### HK Banking Sector — AI Regulatory (HKMA)
- Primary regulator: HKMA (distinct from IA for insurers, SFC for securities)
- No single HK AI law — HKMA issues principles-based circulars; four pillars: senior mgmt responsibility, model risk mgmt, cybersecurity/data risk, third-party provider risk
- HKMA "Fintech 2030" announced Nov 2025 — dedicated AI strategy pillar for authorised institutions; active regulatory build-out
- HKMA GenAI Sandbox (launched Dec 2024): inaugural cohort = HSBC, StanChart, Alibaba Cloud + 10 banks/4 tech firms; trials through mid-2025; HSBC confirmed first real-time customer interaction trials
- HKMA AML circular Nov 2025: 48 AIs surveyed, AI for ML/TF monitoring near-mandatory direction
- HKMA GenAI Sandbox++ (Mar 2026): expanded next phase
- Reliable sources: Freshfields AI tracker, Herbert Smith Freehills AI Tracker HK, HKMA press releases (hkma.gov.hk), Fintech HK (fintechnews.hk)

### HK Insurance Sector — Regulatory
- Primary regulator: Insurance Authority (IA), NOT HKMA. Distinct from banking.
- IA AI posture (as of Oct 2024): principles-based, human oversight required, survey in progress, no hard rules yet
- GL20 (cybersecurity) revised Jan 2025 — active enforcement area
- FSTB AI Policy Statement Oct 2024 applies cross-sector including insurers
- For candidates from banking: HKMA Sandbox credibility transfers well; frame as same governance principles

### HK Insurance Sector — AI Maturity
- AIA is the benchmark for AI maturity in HK life insurance
- TDA 1.0 done (Dec 2023); TDA 2.0 = GenAI embedding
- Reliable benchmarking source: DBS equity research, DigFinGroup
