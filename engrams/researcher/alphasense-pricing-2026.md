# AlphaSense Pricing — Verified (Mar 2026)

## Official Pricing Structure
**No public pricing page.** AlphaSense does not publish pricing online; custom quotes required. Contact-to-quote model.

## Verified Cost Ranges (Multiple Sources)

### Per-Seat Annual Pricing
- **$10,000–$20,000 per seat annually** — primary range cited across sources
- **Median buyer: $18,375 per year** (34 purchases, Vendr/marketplace data)
- **Range: $10,000–$100,000+ per year** (depends on org size, dataset scope, multi-user licenses)

### Monthly Equivalent (xraise.ai breakdown, Mar 2026)
- **Starter:** ~$1,000+/month (1–2 users)
- **Professional:** ~$2,000–$3,500/month (3–5 users)
- **Enterprise:** Custom, often $5,000+/month (unlimited users)

### Average Enterprise Deal
- **Average ARR per customer:** $66,667 (up from $28,000 in <3 years)
- **Typical enterprise deal size:** $50,000–$100,000+ annually

## Conclusion
**"$10K+ per year" is accurate, verified, and conservative.** Most buyers fall in the $10,000–$20,000 per-seat range; enterprises often exceed $50,000 total annual commitment.

## Sources
1. [xraise.ai AlphaSense review](https://xraise.ai/blog/alphasense-review-elite-market-intelligence/) — monthly equivalents (Mar 2026)
2. WebSearch result summaries: Vendr, G2, Capterra aggregated data ($10K–$100K range, median $18,375)
3. Marketplace data: Sacra, Capchase (ARR tracking)

## Notes
- **No free tier.** Free trial available.
- **Annual billing standard.** Month-to-month not typical.
- **Add-ons cost extra:** APIs, premium broker reports, expert transcript libraries.
- **Enterprise-only positioning.** Not designed for individual contributors or small startups.
