### 2. "Agentic AI in Action: Innovating Boldly, Experimenting Responsibly"
- URL: https://www.capco.com/intelligence/capco-intelligence/agentic-ai-in-action

- Authors: Charlotte Byrne, Leo Ferrari, Jessica Forbes (same trio)
- Use cases: Agile delivery acceleration, KYC enhancement, credit decisions, marketing intelligence
- Central message: "unlock new value while staying aligned with risk and regulatory needs"

### 3. "Agentic AI: Transforming Payments & Cash Management"
- URL: https://www.capco.com/intelligence/capco-intelligence/agentic-ai-transforming-payments-and-cash-management
- Date: June 30, 2025
- Authors: Lakshminarayanan Radhakrishnan, Maarten Somers
- Framework: GenAI (content-driven, responsive) vs Agentic AI (autonomous, action-oriented, multi-agent)
- Use cases: Intelligent payment routing, automated reconciliation, dynamic authorization, cash flow forecasting, fraud detection, hyper-personalization
- Implementation challenges: Data security & privacy, regulatory compliance, transparency & explainability, human oversight

### 4. "Transforming Financial Services with Confidence-Driven Agentic AI"
- URL: https://www.capco.com/intelligence/capco-intelligence/transforming-financial-services-with-confidence-driven-agent-ai
- Date: August 25, 2025
- Authors: Chinmoy Bhatiya, Yuxin Ruan
- Framework: Three-tier confidence model:
  - High confidence → auto-progress
  - Moderate confidence → gentle client reminder
  - Low confidence (high-risk) → escalate to human
- Three components: Decision Model + Context Information + Action Rules
- Governance: Legal compliance, explainability, auditability (precise action/decision records)
- Use cases beyond onboarding: KYC/AML, customer service, credit decisions

## Critical Thinking / Lessons-Learned

### 8. "AI in Financial Services: New Frontier, Old Habits?"
- URL: https://www.capco.com/intelligence/capco-intelligence/ai-in-financial-services-new-frontier-old-habits
- Date: January 9, 2026
- Authors: Jessica Forbes, Chris Probert, Joseph Forooghian
- Core argument: Past transformation failures (RPA, Big Data, Agile, Digital) share same pattern — prioritize visible elements, neglect foundations
- Five critical AI principles: Strategic alignment, Process redesign, Foundations, Cross-functional ownership, Responsible AI
- Six strategic decisions before scaling: AI's organizational role, technology adoption boldness, control-vs-pace balance, value creation priorities, build/buy/partner, enterprise scaling
- Killer quote: "AI rewards focus, not frenzy"
- RPA lesson: Used as "substitute for process redesign" — bots mimicked flawed workflows
