## Methodology Notes
- Always try official school fee page first; many are JS-heavy or 403 on WebFetch
- international-schools-database.com is the most reliable aggregator but truncates on WebFetch — use search summaries instead
- SISHK is unusual: debenture is quasi-compulsory (school won't confirm place without it)
- CDNIS and CIS no longer issue new debentures — secondary market only for CDNIS; INR programme still active at CIS
- Harrow capital certificates/debentures fully subscribed — no new issuance
- Always distinguish: annual capital levy (recurring) vs one-off SDL/NCL vs debenture (redeemable capital instrument)
- CIS had an 11% tuition hike for 2025/2026 — highest among top-tier schools
- ESF fees are lower due to EDB government subsidy (recurrent grant); not a direct comparator

## VEO Victoria Kindergarten (Feb 2026)

### Org Structure — CRITICAL DISTINCTION
- **VEO (victoria.edu.hk):** Part of CTF Education Group / New World Development. 9 campuses under VEO brand.
- **Causeway Bay Victoria (cbvictoria.edu.hk):** Separate independent school. Founded 1965. Sponsored by Causeway Bay Kai-Fong Welfare Advance Association. Uses VEO logo/IB PYP but is NOT in the VEO network. Has its own website and separate admissions. Also feeds into VSA.
- Both offer IB PYP. Both give priority to VSA admission. CWB VIC is considered more selective (single campus, less supply).

### VSA Through-Train
- VEO kindergarten graduates get **priority admission** to VSA (Victoria Shanghai Academy)
- 694 VEO graduates admitted to VSA across AY2021/22–2024/25 (4 years combined)
- VSA also gives priority to debenture holders and siblings
- VSA Year 1 annual fees: ~HK$181,200 (2025/26); first year total ~HK$241,700 with one-off fees
- No debenture is required for VEO kindergarten itself — only for VSA priority if not coming via VEO KG

### ESF Comparison
- ESF Kornhill kindergarten opening Aug 2026 — directly competing with VEO in Island East
- ESF KG fees (2025/26): ~HK$109,100–126,600/yr (English to bilingual streams); tuition deposit $9,000 (replaced old debenture)
- ESF KG gives guaranteed entry to ESF primary (through-train). VEO gives priority but not guaranteed entry to VSA.
- ESF = English-medium only (minimal Putonghua). VEO = trilingual (English + Cantonese + Putonghua).
- ESF KG fees similar to VEO mid-range campuses (~$109K–$126K vs VEO $108K–$138K)
