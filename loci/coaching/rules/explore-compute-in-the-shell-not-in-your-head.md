## Compute in the shell, not in your head

If the task requires matching/cross-referencing two lists (e.g., "which modules lack tests"), write a Python one-liner or shell script that does the comparison and outputs the result. Your job is to orchestrate, not to mentally diff 170 items against 150 items.
