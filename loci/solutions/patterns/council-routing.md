# Council Routing: When to Use Frontier Council

## Cognitive/Social Questions Need the Council
Questions with cognitive, social, or behavioural dimensions have more hidden angles than technical questions. Claude underestimates these because it reasons well about logic but poorly about human habits and social dynamics.

**Heuristic:** When a question touches *how humans actually behave* (not just what's optimal), the council is more likely to earn its cost.

## Operational Questions Need Tighter Constraints
When running the council on operational questions (vs strategic decisions), add a prompt constraint: "focus on actionable triggers and concrete rules, not philosophy or abstract cognitive science."

Actual cost ~$0.50/council (not the $2.96 the estimator predicts). GPT-5.2 Pro dominates at 68% of cost.
