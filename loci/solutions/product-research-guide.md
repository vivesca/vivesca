# Product Research Protocol

## Step Zero — How Personal Is This?

- **Universal** (specs matter objectively): research deeply, red-team, compare.
- **Personal** (body/taste/fit): 15 min shortlist max, then go try it in person.

## Process

1. AI search for shortlist only. Switch to human sources (shop staff, forums, owner reviews) for final pick.
2. Never recommend purchase in the same session as research. Time gate: sleep on it.
3. Red-team every frontrunner: search "[product] problems/returns/disappointed".
4. Check manufacturer's official site early — review sites lag by months.
5. Single authoritative source ≠ done. Ask: "Would I trust this if it recommended something I'd never heard of?"

## Visual Analysis

**Requires visual inspection.** When advising on aesthetics (colour matching, keycap pairing, outfit coordination, interior design), fetch and look at product photos before recommending. Text descriptions of colours are unreliable — use the Read tool on images or WebFetch product pages. Don't give visual advice from text alone.
