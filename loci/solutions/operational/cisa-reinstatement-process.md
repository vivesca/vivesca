# CISA Reinstatement: CPE Documentation Process

## Form requirements

ISACA requires the **Fillable Verification Attendance Form** — one per year. Generic training records or Workday printouts are NOT sufficient.

Each form needs:
- **CISA certification number** (8 digits, from ISACA member portal) — goes on every form. Gather this FIRST before filling any forms.
- **Verifier signature** — the person who can confirm you attended the training. Typically your manager or course coordinator.
- **Course description** — keep it concise to avoid text overflow in the form field.

## Common pitfalls

1. **Description text overflow:** After filling forms (programmatically or in Word), visually inspect for text overflow — especially the course description field. Print a test page before printing all 7+.
2. **Cert number placeholder:** Don't leave as `[PENDING]` — you'll have to regenerate all forms.
3. **Wrong documentation format:** The specific ISACA fillable form is mandatory. No substitutes.

## Submission

Mail completed forms to ISACA. Processing takes several weeks.
