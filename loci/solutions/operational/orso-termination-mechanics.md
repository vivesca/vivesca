# ORSO Termination: MMB vs Excess

When leaving an employer with an ORSO scheme (e.g. CNCBI via Fidelity), the payout has two legally distinct portions:

## MMB (Minimum MPF Benefit)

- **Must be preserved** in an MPF scheme — cannot be taken as cash.
- Roll over to your personal MPF account.
- Example: HSBC MPF SuperTrust Plus, account 44692794.

## Excess (above MMB)

- **Can be cashed out** to a bank account.
- Example: HSBC 512-846551-833.

## Process

1. HR triggers the termination with the ORSO provider (Fidelity).
2. Fill the Fidelity termination form: specify MMB destination (MPF scheme) and excess destination (bank account).
3. MMB portion rolls over; excess is deposited.

## Timing

Processing takes several weeks after last day. Don't expect immediate payout.
