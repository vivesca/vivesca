# HK Rates (差餉地租): PPS Payment Gotchas

## Double-payment credits forward automatically

If you accidentally double-pay quarterly rates via PPS, the overpayment credits to the next quarter automatically. No action needed.

## PPS takes 2-3 working days to appear

PPS payments take 2-3 working days to show on the GovHK 綜合帳目 portal. FPS is near-instant. If checking after a PPS payment, wait 3 working days before assuming it failed.
