# Resignation & Exit Tactics (HK Banking)

Reusable patterns from CNCBI exit. Full context: [[HK Banking Resignation Tactics]], [[Capco Transition]].

## PILON

- **HR won't calculate in advance.** Self-estimate: 21 days × (annual base ÷ 365). Don't wait for HR — they can only calculate after the month's payroll closes.
- **Don't nudge HRSS in the first two weeks of the month** — they genuinely can't calculate yet.

## Disclosure

- **Don't name new employer** until employment formally ends or PILON is settled. "Consulting" is sufficient.
- **WhatsApp approval ≠ HR system approval.** Follow up to get agreed dates into Workday/HRMS.

## CYA Communications

- **Match normal communication patterns.** A never-before-sent comprehensive status update screams "paper trail." Embed CYA moves inside existing scheduled channels (e.g. monthly meeting invite reply).

## Background checks (FADV)

- During active employment (no leaving letter yet), submit the **new employer's offer letter with salary redacted** (via pymupdf). Note in the reply that you're still employed. Pay stub follows on next payroll date.

## Competency/interview forms

- If HR asks you to fill a Workday competency form for a candidate you didn't interview, clarify in writing that you weren't the assessor. Wait for the actual interviewer to handle it.
