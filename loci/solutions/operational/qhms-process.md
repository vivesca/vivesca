# QHMS Body Check Process

## Booking

- **No Sunday booking** — app is unavailable on Sundays.
- Use WhatsApp **8301 8301** on a weekday morning instead.
- Location: 海富中心一期 16F 1605-09室, Admiralty.

## Prep

- **Fasting:** 8 hours (no food/coffee after midnight, water only). This is for blood tests.
- **Stool sample (FIT):** No dietary restrictions — it's an immunochemical test, not chemical. Fibre just makes collection easier. Collect at home, return to clinic within 48h.
- **Bring:** HKID, mention relevant medical history.

## SMS prep links are broken

QHMS sends `rebrand.ly` short links in SMS — these hit Cloudflare blocks. Go directly to `qhms.com` for preparation instructions.
