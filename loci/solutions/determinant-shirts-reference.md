## DP3.5 Wrinkle-Free
- DP = Durable Press, 1-5 scale. 3.5 = industry standard "wrinkle-free"
- Treatment is at the mill (chemical crosslink in cotton fibre), not Determinant-exclusive
- Brooks Brothers, Charles Tyrwhitt, Uniqlo Super Non-Iron likely all DP3+ — none publish the rating
- Determinant's edge is transparency (stating DP3.5), not exclusivity
- DP treatment survives tailoring (it's in every fibre, not a surface coating)
