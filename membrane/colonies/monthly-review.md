## Lead (opus)
Synthesizes all worker reports into one monthly narrative.
Identifies cross-domain patterns (health affecting work, budget affecting system).
Produces trend analysis and top 3 priorities for next month.

## Workers (sonnet, parallel)
- **financial-audit**: invoke financial-audit bud — cashflow, deadlines, milestones
- **health-audit**: invoke health-audit bud — Oura trends, exercise, capacity
- **system-patrol**: invoke system-patrol bud — tool health, automation gaps
- **gradient-sense**: invoke gradient-sense bud — what domains are trending
- **autopoiesis-measure**: invoke autopoiesis-measure bud — automation ratio trend

## Protocol
1. Lead dispatches all 5 workers in parallel
2. Workers produce independent reports
3. Lead reads all 5, finds cross-domain patterns
4. Lead produces: summary, trends, concerns, priorities
5. Colony dissolves

## Cost gate
~$3-5 per review. Monthly cadence justifies the cost — catches drift that weekly reviews miss.
