---
name: landscape-survey
description: Comprehensive survey of a domain — multiple facets scanned in parallel, synthesized into one reference document.
product: landscape reference doc with structured findings, key players, trends, and open questions
trigger: entering a new domain, or a known domain has shifted enough that prior knowledge is stale
---

## Lead (opus)
Defines the survey frame before dispatching: what questions must the reference doc answer?
Synthesizes worker findings into one coherent reference — not a concatenation, a narrative.
Identifies where workers' findings contradict or reinforce each other.

## Workers (sonnet, parallel — 3-5 depending on domain breadth)
Assign one worker per distinct facet of the domain. Typical split:
- **history-and-context**: how did this domain get here? Key shifts, defining moments, legacy constraints
- **current-state**: who are the main actors? What are the dominant approaches? What is actually in production?
- **emerging-signals**: what is nascent? What are practitioners arguing about? Where is the energy?
- **adjacent-domains**: what neighboring domains are influencing or being influenced by this one?
- **failure-modes**: what has not worked? What cautionary cases exist? What does the graveyard look like?

## Protocol
1. Lead writes the survey frame: 5-7 questions the reference doc must answer
2. Lead assigns facets to workers, giving each the survey frame so they know what the synthesis needs
3. Workers run in parallel — each produces a structured findings brief (not a full doc, just the evidence)
4. Lead reads all worker briefs, identifies cross-facet patterns, contradictions, and gaps
5. Lead produces the reference doc: framed narrative, not a list of findings
6. Lead adds an "open questions" section — what the survey could not resolve
7. Colony dissolves

## Cost gate
~$3-6 depending on domain complexity and number of workers. Justified when: the domain will affect decisions across multiple projects, or prior knowledge is more than 6 months stale. Single-question domain checks = single bud with web search.

## Dissolution rule
Dissolves after the reference doc is written to the vault. Open questions section is mandatory — a survey without unknowns is overconfident.
