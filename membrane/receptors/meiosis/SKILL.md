# Quarterly Review

First Saturday of March, June, September, December — after `/ecdysis` and `/mitosis`. Am I on track?

## Triggers

- quarterly
- quarterly review
- q1 review
- q2 review
- q3 review
- q4 review

## Sections

### 1. Direction audit

Read `~/epigenome/chromatin/Life OS.md`. For each domain ask:
- Is this still the right framing?
- Has anything shifted?
- Is the listed "next step" still relevant or stale?

Update the note in-place — it's a live map, not a historical document.

### 2. Career trajectory

Read `memory/user_career_north_star.md`. Check:
- Is this quarter's work building toward "real AI problems + sharp people who build"?
- Flag drift — are you getting pulled into busywork, or staying on the sharp edge?
- Any credential milestones hit or missed? (GARP, AIGP, ECF timeline)
- Networking: who's in motion, who needs follow-up? Any new contacts worth cultivating?

### 3. Financial health

- **Emergency fund** — still 6 months of expenses? Any large upcoming costs?
- **Tax provision** — enough set aside for salaries tax?
- **Credit card audit** — any cards with annual fees not earning their keep? (Mox consolidation progress)
- **Mortgage rate** — any significant market shift since last check? (SCB prime, best market cap)
- **Insurance** — AIA plan adequate? Term life in place? Any gaps?
- **NLSFT quarterly payment** — confirm paid or upcoming

### 4. Investment review

- **Portfolio check** — log into IBKR, check values. No tinkering.
- **Rebalancing** — only if >5% drift from target allocation
- **Irish UCITS availability** — check if any new factor funds launched (SCV gap)
- **Tax efficiency** — any US estate exposure remaining? (Schwab → IBKR migration status)

### 5. Yearly admin (Q4 only — December)

Skip in Q1-Q3. In Q4, add:
- **ISACA CPEs** — logged before December deadline?
- **SCB credit card annual fee** — waived this year?
- **Will/estate planning** — still current? Any life changes?
- **Insurance** — annual coverage review
- **Investment strategy** — annual check (fund options, allocation drift)
- **Credential plan** — what's next year's certification target?
- **Start / Stop / Continue** — what to change next year?

### 6. Quarterly note

Create `~/epigenome/chromatin/Quarterly/YYYY-QX.md`:

```markdown
# YYYY QX Review

## Direction
[What shifted, what held]

## Career
[Trajectory check — on track / drifting / course correction needed]

## Finances
[Health summary — emergency fund, tax, debt, insurance]

## Investments
[Portfolio value, allocation, any action taken]

## Next Quarter Focus
1. [Primary]
2. [Secondary]
3. [Watch]
```

## Boundaries

- Do NOT execute trades or financial transactions — flag and let Terry act
- Do NOT run weekly/monthly items — those already ran
- Keep it to 30 minutes. Depth belongs in follow-up sessions.
