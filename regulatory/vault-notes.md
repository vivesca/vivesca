## Vault Note Conventions

- One concern per note. Title needs "and" → split.
- Every note: `**Related:**` line with `[[wikilinks]]`.
- Facts age, rules don't. Dates/amounts → vault notes, not CLAUDE.md.
- `**Update (Month X):**` headers for dated changes.
- Rename/move with wikilink update: `obsidian vault="notes" rename file="name" name="new-name"`.
- Backlinks: `obsidian vault="notes" backlinks file="name"`.
- Depth: see `~/epigenome/chromatin/Reference/vault/note-structure.md`.
